I love games, especially mobile games. whenever i want to play any kind of game i always look for games that are actually worthwhile. either story , action, puzzles or just overall a great game.

Today i will give my TOP 10 best Android games that i found on my journey to pass the time some games feature story some action and some are infintely replayable.


10. Gladiator Manager

This game has a sweet spot in my heart because of its art style and lots of replayable content .The fact that all ingame purchases can also be bought with just your skills is a superb idea and i am all for it. Gladiator Manager is a game about well managing gladiators, you can build your residence add training grounds and buy blacksmiths and medicus for your personal gladiators. going to your ludus you can access these gladiator friends and upgrade their stats to whichever build you prefer.

There are 6 specializations with different benefits, their are also different traits your gladiators can get as well as weapons and armor they can equip. At the market you can but gladiators and beasts as well like lion , wolf , elephants too which is really cool and weapons/armors are available for purchase with coins.You can register gladiators to tournaments and win fame coins and items.

9/10 A Great Game
